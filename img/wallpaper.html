Desktop wallpapers by Canva

Enough of the generic peaceful landscape, swirling galaxies, or abstract geometric patterns! Jazz up your computer, phone or tablet by creating a wallpaper design unique to your taste and style. It's easy and fun to make wallpapers for any device through Canva's collection of wallpaper templates and suite of editing tools. Plus, you can access your design anytime on your computer, iPad and iPhone.

Want to be greeted by your favorite band, celebrity, or pictures of family and friends the moment you log in? Or maybe you want to see your favorite selfie on your screen — because why not? Just drag and drop your photo and Canva will upload and save it for you. For businesses and brands, you can upload your logo and use it to create a wallpaper for company-issued devices. Once the photo is uploaded, you can access and use it anytime! Or, browse through Canva's incredible image library which contains more than a million professionally-shot stock images, illustrations and graphics.

Think a photo needs some tweaking? Try out filters like Cali, Festive or Epic. You can even add blur, vignette and x-process effects. Framing and composition are no sweat because every image in Canva can easily be cropped, scaled and placed within grids and frames.

With the text tool, you can leave yourself motivational messages or your favorite quote. There are over a hundred fonts to choose from plus text holders to help you create some truly impressive typography. Download a high resolution copy of your wallpaper or share it on your Facebook or Twitter profile.
